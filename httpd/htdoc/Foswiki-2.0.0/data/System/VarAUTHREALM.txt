---+ AUTHREALM -- authentication realm
String defined as the ={AuthRealm}= expert option in [[%SCRIPTURLPATH{"configure"}%][configure]] =Security And Authentication= tab, =Login sub-tab.. This is used in certain password encodings, and in login templates as part of the login prompt.
