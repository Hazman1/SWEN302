---+++ Deprecated macros

    "parent.name='DeprecatedMacros'"
    type="query"
    topic="Var*"
    casesensitive="on"
    web="%SYSTEMWEB%"

    nonoise="on"
}%

---++ Shortcuts

%SEARCH{
    "^      \* Shortcut [A-Z]+ --"
    web="%SYSTEMWEB%"
    topic="DefaultPreferences"
    multiple="on"
    type="regex"
    format="   * $pattern(.*?Shortcut ([^\n]+).*)"
    nonoise="on"
}%
For example, =%<nop>CARET%= will appear as =%CARET%=
